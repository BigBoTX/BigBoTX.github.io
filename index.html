The Present.

The Houston Texans kicked off the current NFL season with high hopes and aspirations under the leadership of their newly aqquired coaching staff and key players. The aquistion of new head coach: Demeco Ryans excited Texans fans, as his expertise and track record of success is nothing to overlook. His free agent signings have been amazing, however his major talent growth came through the NFL draft. With the number 2 and 3 overall picks, The Houston Texans drafted CJ Stroud, QB from OSU and Will Anderson, DE from ALABAMA. These rookies have proven themselves within the league and showed their potential as young superstars, especially CJ Stroud, as he is having the greatest QB Rookie season ever. Furthermore, the team's roster saw some notable changes during the offseason, with new additions aiming to bolster both the offensive and defensive sides of the ball. The coaching staff implemented strategic adjustments to enhance the team's overall performance and address areas of improvement from the previous season. In terms of on-field performance, the Houston Texans face a challenging schedule, engaging in intense matchups against formidable opponents. The team showcased resilience and determination, with standout performances from key players contributing to some memorable moments on the field. As the season progressed, fans eagerly watched as the Texans navigated the highs and lows, with each game presenting an opportunity for the team to demonstrate its potential and competitive spirit. The atmosphere of Texans fans are rejuvinated and excited as The Texans sit at a very respectable 5-4 Win to loss record, almost double the amount of wins of just one year ago. (3-14) Despite the challenges, the Houston Texans remained focused on their goals for the season. Whether securing crucial victories or learning from setbacks, the team continued to evolve, showing glimpses of their future potential. The support from the dedicated fan base remained unwavering, as fans cheered on the Texans through every thrilling touchdown and crucial defensive stop. As the season approached its conclusion, the Houston Texans aimed to finish strong, leaving a lasting impression on the league and setting the stage for what promises to be an exciting future for the team.
